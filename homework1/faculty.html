Michael Fredericks

Assistant Professor of Computer Science (269) 749-7628

Michael Fredericks, assistant professor of computer science, has worked at Olivet College since 2002.Fredericks teaches a variety of computer courses at Olivet, including computer information systems, Web design, spreadsheet/database systems and a self and community course. With extensive knowledge of Macromedia Dreamweaver and Flash applications, Fredericks owns and operates his own Web site design company. Fredericks has a bachelor's degree in business administration from Northwood University, a master's degree in information systems administration from Central Michigan University and certification in the Microsoft Office Suite. Prior to joining Olivet, he worked at Lansing Community College as an academic adviser.
